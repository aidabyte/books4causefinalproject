Our Story

In 2009, Yosef Lifchitz began Books4Cause, Inc. and began managing books and electronic donations drives in Chicago, New York City, and college campuses.

Thanks to your donations, Books4Cause is able to help those in need of literacy resources. As of today, Books4Cause has helped create over 120 libraries across Africa and distributed books to those in need across Chicago through our Free Library.
